Line Following Robot

For one of final labs in ME430, we made a line-following robot to go around a track. We used two IR sensors, two seven-segment displays, as well as a two DC motors. Everything that could go wrong went wrong when bulding ours - even our breadboard burned out. It could not have been more gratifying when we finally got it to work!
