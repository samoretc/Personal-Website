Data Mining

For the final project in our Data Mining Course, we decided we wanted use the power of big data to help solve a real world (and familiar) problem: predicting flight delays. To do this, we obtained a data set including information about all flights leaving San Francisco International Airport (SFO) over the past two years and tried to make predictions.
